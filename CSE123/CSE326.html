Empowering Education Initiatives

On the occasion of World Literacy Day, Gurgaon-based NGO Heart joins hands with the District Public Libraries in Gurgaon, Bhiwani, and Karnal for the noble cause of promoting literacy and education within our community. Close to 500 books were distributed among the specified areas.

Supporting Local Communities

In an altruistic collaboration, Go Spiritual India and Approach Entertainment have initiated a Blanket Donation Campaign as the winter season blankets North India. This campaign aims to orchestrate blanket, clothing, and food donation drives for the underprivileged in major cities of North India.
